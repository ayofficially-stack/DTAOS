# White-label Design

## Purpose

Define the standard design principles for creating reusable B2B sales materials.

The objective is to enable partners to reuse the material by replacing only their company identity while preserving the technical value.

---

## Rule

Design for reuse.

Separate technology from branding.

The material should remain effective even when the company logo is replaced.

---

## Standard Principles

### Neutral Branding

Use a neutral visual identity.

Avoid excessive company-specific branding.

---

### Editable Deliverables

The final deliverable must be an editable PowerPoint.

PDF is for distribution.

PowerPoint is for collaboration.

---

### Industrial Luxury

Maintain a clean, premium, and engineering-oriented design language.

Professionalism is more important than decoration.

---

### Real Photography

Use real products, real equipment, and real validation whenever possible.

Real images build technical credibility.

---

### Information First

Information hierarchy is more important than visual decoration.

Design should guide understanding, not compete with the content.

---

## Why

White-label materials are intended to be reused by multiple partners.

A reusable design system reduces production cost while increasing consistency and scalability.

A neutral design allows the technology to remain the primary focus.

---

## Reuse

- Partner Sales Materials
- OEM/ODM Sales
- Open Innovation
- Government Proposals
- Technical Brochures
- Conference Materials
- Website Contents
