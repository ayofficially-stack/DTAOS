# Editorial Design

## Purpose

Define the editorial design principles for B2B technical communication.

The objective of editorial design is to improve understanding, not decoration.

---

## Rule

Do not redesign information.

Redesign the way information is understood.

Every design decision must improve clarity, readability, and credibility.

---

## Editorial Principles

### Preserve

The following elements represent the core value of the original content and should be preserved.

- Information Hierarchy
- Engineering Logic
- Technical Accuracy
- Real Photography
- Quantitative Data

---

### Improve

The following elements should be refined to improve communication.

- Typography
- Grid System
- Alignment
- White Space
- Editorial Rhythm
- Visual Consistency

---

### Avoid

Avoid elements that reduce clarity or distract from the message.

- Decorative Icons
- Visual Noise
- Unnecessary Graphics
- Excessive Colors
- Recreating Information
- Decoration without Purpose

---

## Why

Editorial design is not about making documents look different.

Editorial design is about making technical information easier to understand, faster to read, and more convincing.

Good editorial design increases trust without changing technical content.
