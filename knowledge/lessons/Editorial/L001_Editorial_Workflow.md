# Editorial Workflow

## Purpose

Define the standard editorial workflow for producing high-quality technical deliverables.

The workflow prioritizes information quality before visual quality.

---

## Rule

Do not create the final deliverable first.

Validate the structure before refining the design.

Prototype and Deliverable are different stages with different purposes.

---

## Standard Workflow

Requirements

↓

Information Architecture

↓

Prototype

↓

Editorial Review

↓

Revision

↓

Editable Deliverable

↓

Quality Review

↓

Release

---

## Why

Building the final PowerPoint from the beginning leads to unnecessary revisions and inconsistent quality.

Separating planning, prototyping, editorial review, and production significantly improves efficiency while reducing revision costs.

Editorial quality is achieved through iteration, not through the first draft.
