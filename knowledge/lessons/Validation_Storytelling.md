# Validation Storytelling

## Purpose

Define the standard storytelling structure for presenting technical validation.

The objective is not to report test results, but to communicate validated value.

---

## Rule

Validation is not a report.

Validation is proof.

Present evidence before explanation.

Present business impact before technical details.

---

## Standard Flow

Validation Result

↓

Case Study

↓

Key Metrics (KPI)

↓

Technical Comparison

↓

Business Value

---

## Why

Technical validation should answer one question first.

"Can this technology be trusted?"

Showing validated results before detailed explanations increases credibility and improves decision making.

People remember evidence better than specifications.

---

## Reuse

- White-label Sales Sheets
- Government Proposals
- NET Certification
- Technical Brochures
- Conference Presentations
- IR Decks
